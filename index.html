About Us

Private enterprise O.C. Radical Timbers is based in Harare Home Industry. We provide roofing timber, ceiling boards, doors, crating timber, cover strips, bolts and nuts, fascia boards, nails and other building materials. We also supply and repair various kinds of roofs.
